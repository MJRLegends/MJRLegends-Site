Space Astronomy 1

Space Astronomy is a space based modpack, that include loads of the latest tech and space mods. Explore the Universe with the help of the provided quest book that will lead you around and that required steps that you will need to take. This mod pack is also on the FTB Launcher!

You can find the Server files on the FTB launcher or on the main menu of the pack!
